  Hand-check of discount mechanism:
    not/1 decl (arity 1) = 3 bits; amortised once k>=1.

    Growing |F| lowers log2(|F|) per-occurrence charge.
    Net discount per prior = ~23.68 bits (from discount_per_step above).

ANALYSIS C: Scope statement
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Bits-mode selectivity holds in lean-context (k=0 prior exception structures): the N=3+1exc pattern is rejected with delta_bits=-69.02. After 0 prior exception-bearing accepts, the same proposal flips to accepted (delta_bits=-69.02). The amortisation erosion is approx 23.68 bits per prior structure. The noise-filter guarantee from EX32 strictly applies to the first generalization proposals encountered in ordering (lean context). For orderings that process the true pattern first, not/1 and exception functors are amortised before noise proposals are evaluated; in this regime spurious N=3+1exc groups can flip from reject to accept.
